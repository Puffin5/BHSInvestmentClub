World Youth Investment Conference

At the World Youth Investment Conference, schools engage in multiple competitions and challenges, earning points based on their performance. Throughout the year, each school strives for excellence, building their investment knowledge and refining their financial strategies. The top 10 schools at the end of the regular season earn an opportunity to compete in the final live virtual showdown, where the winner is crowned.

ICOA (Investing Clubs of America) Competitions

ICOA is an investing club community offering a variety of resources and challenges. They are currently offering two competitions including a stock analysis and index fund competition. Participants can earn money and awards by completing a paper following the specificed guidelines.
